What Is Mesa?

MESA, Mathematics, Engineering, Science, and Acknowledgements, is an afterschool program that gives STEM students the opportunity to get good exposure and preparation they need in order to suceed in an engineering enviroment. MESA students use the Engineering Design Process with every project they do
