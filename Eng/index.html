JAMES WEBB SPACE TELESCOPE

The James Webb Space Telescope is the worlds largest, most powerful and complex telescope ever to be made and launched into space. It has the potential to fundamentally alter what we know of the universe already. It was built and made to succede the Hubble Space Telescope. The JWST was made by NASA with contribuation from the Europen and Canadian Space Agency. The Telescope was named James E. Webb, who was the administrator of NASA at some point and played an integral role in the Apollo program.

ENGINEERING MARVEL

The JWST is such an amazing engineering marvel. It has the capability to determine how galaxies evolved from their formation until now, search for the first galaxies or luminous objects formed after the Big Bang and so much more. My love and passion for Math and Science just couldn't let me ignore this telescope. I insetly feel in love with its gold-plated eighteen hexagonal mirror segments.Space has been a passion of mine since i was a little kid. I used to spend hours reading books and watching videos regarding the universe. I hope to be able to create something even better than this in my future years.
